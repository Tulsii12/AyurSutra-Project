About Our Center

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2025, our Panchakarma Center has been a trusted hub of traditional Ayurvedic healing. For over two decades, we have dedicated ourselves to preserving and practicing authentic Panchakarma therapies.

With a team of qualified Ayurvedic doctors and experienced therapists, we provide personalized treatments that nurture the mind, body, and spirit — offering holistic care tailored to each individual’s needs.
